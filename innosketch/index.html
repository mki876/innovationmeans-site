How it works

  • Press N or click Add Node to add ideas.
  • Choose Connect, click two nodes to draw an arrow.
  • Use Cluster to group nodes inside a dashed area.
  • Export with PNG or PDF. Save as .innos.
  • Toggle Contrast for a darker high-focus theme.

Made for real-world use

  • Students: research outlines, essay planning, revision maps.
  • Founders: product ideas, lean canvases, pitch structures.
  • Teams: campaign plans, user journeys, topic clusters for SEO.

FAQ

Does it work without internet?

Yes. After the page loads once, all features run locally in your browser.

Where are my maps stored?

On your device. You can export .innos files and reopen them later.

Is any data collected?

No. InnoSketch is privacy-friendly: no logins, analytics, or ads.
